Our Story

In mid-April 2025, we hosted a bioinformatics agent training lecture to help beginners with zero background get started in bioinformatics data analysis.

Following that, we organized a Bioinformatics Agent Competition using our BioMaster product, where undergraduate students with no biology background were able to professionally process population genetics data.
